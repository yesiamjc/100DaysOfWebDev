Lata Mangeshkar

The nightingale of India

Here's a timeline of Lata Ji

  • 1929- Born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh on September 28.
  • 1942- She started her singing career by singing for a Marathi film.
  • 1945- Moved to Mumbai.
  • 1948- Master Vinayak, her early mentor passed away.
  • 1950's- Lata had become a popular playback singer in Bollywood.
  • 1969- She won the Padma Bhushan.
  • 1974- Mangeshkar went international. She was the first Indian to perform at the Royal Albert Hall, London.
  • 1993- Bestowed with Life time achievement award.
  • 1999- She was honored with the Padma Vibhushan.
  • 2001- Winner of the Bharat Ratna award.
  • 2009- ANR National Award.
  • 2015- Mangeshkar's last song for a film was 'Jeena Hai Kya' for 'Dunno Y2... Life Is A Moment', a sequel to the Kapil Sharma-starrer 'Dunno Y... Na Jaane Kyun'. She recorded the song at her own studio.
  • 2022- India's Nightingale Lata Mangeshkar died at 92 on February 6. Her death was attributed to multiple organ failure after COVID as per Breach Candy hospital officials.
